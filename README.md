# Mini Windmill Power Generation

## Overview
This project is a mini windmill based renewable energy system designed to generate power for smart traffic signal lighting. It demonstrates how wind energy can be converted into electrical energy and used for small-scale smart applications.

## System Architecture

The system converts wind energy into electrical energy using a mini windmill connected to a DC generator.  
The generated electricity is stored in a battery and used to power traffic signal LEDs.

## Working Principle

1. Wind rotates the windmill blades.
2. The windmill drives a DC generator.
3. The generator converts mechanical energy to electrical energy.
4. The electricity is stored in a battery.
5. Stored energy powers the traffic signal LEDs.
